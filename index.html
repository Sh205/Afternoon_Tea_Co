Afternoon Tea at Yours

Founded in 2017, The Tea People is proud to bring you the first at home afternoon tea service to St Andrews. Each product is deliciously homemade using the finest organic and ethically sourced artisan ingredients. Explore the magical world of loose-leaf tea through a truely unique and hastle-free alternative to the traditional party.


Catering For You

The Tea People is committed to providing gluten-free, nut-free, and vegan options for all your dietary needs. You will be able to customise your afternoon tea selection, from cakes to your favourite type of tea.


How Does it Work?

Simply order an afternoon tea package from our website one week in advance of the party and we will deliver it on the day of your celebration. We will provide you with all the necessary utensils and glassware for your party and pick up the next day. No mess and no washing-up.
